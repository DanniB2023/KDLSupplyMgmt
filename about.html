About KDL Services


KDL Services is a recently launched minority-owned business in Louisville, Kentucky.



Our central location allows access to 2/3 of the US population within one days drive. We are also located 3 miles of the UPS Worldport Hub, creating another strategic advantage.



The primary focus of KDL Services is to provide logistical services, related to print, packaging, shipping/receiving of goods, order fulfillment and storage. Here at KDL Services we pride ourselves on our ability to adapt and adjust to any project no matter the size and/or complexity. When it comes to dependability, there is not another company that you would rather have on your side. Expect us to complete and surpass every potential need given to us without compromising the quality and service that we provide.



Owner/Entreprenur
